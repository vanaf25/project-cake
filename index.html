Keto Birthday Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups blanched almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup butter use dairy free, if desired
  • 1 cup granulated sweetener of choice
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup almond milk can use any milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 serving keto vanilla frosting

Nutrition Facts

Serving: 1slice | Calories: 172kcal | Carbohydrates: 5g | Protein: 4g | Fat: 16g | Sodium: 209mg | Potassium: 20mg | Fiber: 3g | Vitamin A: 333IU | Calcium: 75mg | Iron: 1mg | NET CARBS: 2g

Notes

* Erythritol or monk fruit sweetener.
** I haven't tried using flax eggs or chia eggs. I tried this with egg replacer, and the texture was too dense.
